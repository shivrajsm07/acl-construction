Land Surveying

Overview

Our Land Surveying service provides precise measurement and mapping of land properties using advanced surveying equipment. We deliver accurate data essential for construction planning, property transactions, and development projects.

What We Offer

  • Boundary surveys
  • Topographic surveys
  • Construction surveys
  • GPS mapping
  • Property subdivision
  • As-built surveys
  • Digital terrain modeling

Types of Surveys

  • Property boundary surveys
  • Elevation certificates
  • Site planning surveys
  • Subdivision surveys
  • ALTA/NSPS surveys
  • Construction staking

Our Survey Process

  1. Initial consultation and requirement analysis
  2. Research of existing records and documents
  3. Field survey and data collection
  4. Data processing and analysis
  5. Map preparation and drafting
  6. Quality control review
  7. Final deliverable preparation

Equipment We Use

  • Total stations
  • GPS/GNSS receivers
  • Digital levels
  • 3D laser scanners
  • Drone mapping technology
  • Advanced CAD software

Benefits of Professional Land Surveying

  • Accurate property boundaries
  • Legal compliance
  • Dispute prevention
  • Informed development decisions
  • Precise construction planning
